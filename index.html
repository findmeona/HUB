What We Do

Our Signature Services

🌸

Chhab Decoration

છાબ શણગાર

Beautifully decorated Chhab (ceremonial tray) arrangements with fresh flowers, traditional motifs, and premium accessories for weddings and pre-wedding rituals.

🌿

Vana Rasam

વન-રસ્મ

Elegant Vana ceremony setups with leafy greenery, floral backdrops, and traditional Gujarati elements that honor this sacred pre-wedding ritual.

📜

Lagna Lekhan

લગ્ન-લેખન

Artistically decorated invitation setups and ceremonial letter presentations, making your Lagna Lekhan moment as grand as the wedding itself.

🌙

Chhathi Decoration

છઠ્ઠી ઉત્સવ

Warm, joyful décor for the Chhathi (6th day baby ceremony) — pastel themes, floral cradles, welcome gates, and personalized backdrops for the new arrival.

🪷

Kanku Pagla

કંકુ-પગલા

Sacred and stunning Kanku Pagla (Griha Pravesh footstep) décor — from threshold flower arrangements to full home entrance decoration welcoming Goddess Lakshmi.

🎊

Traditional Gujarati Events

ગુજરાતી ઉત્સવ

Full-scale decoration for Garba nights, Navratri, Diwali, housewarming, and all Gujarati cultural celebrations with authentic regional style and modern flair.
